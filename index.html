Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

iMuslim360 | دليل المسلم is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Quick Summary: iMuslim360 does not collect, store, or share your personal information. All data remains on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

What we collect: Approximate location (city/region level)

Why we collect it: To calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your geographic location

How we use it:

  • Used exclusively for prayer time calculations using astronomical algorithms
  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings

2.2 User Preferences & Bookmarks

What we collect: Your app settings, bookmarked Quran verses, reading history

How we store it:

  • Stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and Core Data
  • Never synced to cloud or external servers
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

2.3 Crash Reports (Optional)

What we collect: Anonymous crash data if you opt-in to share analytics with Apple

How we use it:

  • To identify and fix bugs
  • To improve app stability
  • This is controlled by iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

MyQuran does not collect, access, or store:

  • ❌ Name, email, or phone number
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Photos or camera data
  • ❌ Microphone or audio recordings
  • ❌ Precise GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Health or fitness data
  • ❌ Financial information (in-app purchases handled by Apple)
  • ❌ Social media accounts
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Device identifiers for advertising

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we process is used solely to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • Save your bookmarks and reading preferences
  • Remember your app settings (language, theme, notification preferences)
  • Provide prayer time notifications if you enable them

All processing happens locally on your device.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share your data with anyone.

5.1 Third-Party Services

iMuslim360 uses the following iOS system services:

  • Apple Location Services: For prayer time calculation (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases/donations (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple User Notifications: For prayer reminders (local notifications only)

5.2 No Analytics or Advertising

  • ✅ No Google Analytics
  • ✅ No Facebook SDK
  • ✅ No advertising networks
  • ✅ No user tracking
  • ✅ No data brokers

6. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • No data transmitted over the internet (except initial Quran data download)
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • No backend servers storing your information

7. Data Retention & Deletion

7.1 How Long We Keep Data

Data is kept indefinitely on your device until you:

  • Delete the app (all data automatically deleted)
  • Reset app data in Settings
  • Clear specific bookmarks or preferences

7.2 How to Delete Your Data

To completely remove all iMuslim360 data:

  1. Uninstall the app from your device
  2. All local data will be permanently deleted

8. Children's Privacy

iMuslim360 is suitable for all ages, including children. We do not:

  • Knowingly collect information from children
  • Require personal information to use the app
  • Display advertisements or third-party content
  • Enable social sharing or communication features

Parents can safely allow children to use MyQuran without privacy concerns.

9. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Location Permission: Grant or revoke in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → iMuslim360
  • Notifications: Enable or disable in iOS Settings → Notifications → iMuslim360
  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Access Data: All your data is stored in the app's interface (bookmarks, settings)

10. In-App Purchases

iMuslim360 offers optional donations through Apple's In-App Purchase system:

  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Purchase history is managed by Apple through your Apple ID
  • For billing inquiries, contact Apple Support

11. International Users

  • No data residency concerns
